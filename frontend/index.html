Graph View (Neo4j/Bloom)

In the full integration, this area will show a Neo4j graph (e.g., via Bloom).

How to read this graph

  • Orange: subject account (alerted)
  • Blue: other accounts receiving funds from subject
  • Teal: devices the subject uses (shared devices indicate risk)
  • Yellow: transactions originating from the subject
  • Edges: USES (account → device), PERFORMS (account → transaction), TO (transaction → destination account)

Pain point addressed: quickly spot mule patterns and shared devices without running Cypher manually—analysts get a visual of who the alerted account pays and which devices link them.
